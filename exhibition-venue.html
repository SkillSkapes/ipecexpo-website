Exhibition Venue

HITEX (Hyderabad International Trade Expositions Ltd.) is an ideal venue for B2B and B2C exhibitions and also for the combination of both. Vast indoor and outdoor event areas offer ample space to plan your exhibitions and trade fairs of all sizes.

HITEX brings forth futuristic Conference halls, Meeting rooms and Customizable spaces with advanced audiovisual & broadcasting technology, flexible seating and ambient space - making all your Product launches, Brand revitalizations, Boardroom discussions, Business meetings, Seminars or Large scale international conferences simple and easy.

HITEX provides a unique set-up to those brands wanting to move a step forward towards generating greater value by hosting corporate events, musical events or annual general meetings with ease. It is a multi-purpose platform to showcase your business.

HITEX's 53 acres of prime land with 30,000 sq.m of indoor, 10,500 sq.m of outdoor and 5000 sq.m of fair park and parking space facility helps you host all your B2B or B2C exhibitions at your comfort!
